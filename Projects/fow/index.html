What is Force of Will?


Force of Will is a Japanese fantasy series centering around two major pieces, a trading card game, and the story articles. These two pieces come together to form a game and universe rife with fun, drama, and customization!



The Force of Will Products

Booster Box - Each booster box contains 36 Booster Packs. Additionally, each box contains a pack of magic stones and basic rulers to help you draft and get started!
