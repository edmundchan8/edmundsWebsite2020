Medicare

Medicare is a federal health insurance program. It is for:


  • People over 65 years old.
  • Certain young people with disabilities.
  • People with End-Stage Renal Disease (permanent kidney failure requiring dialysis or a transplant, sometimes called ESRD).

There are 4 parts of medicare that provide insurance for different aspects of healthcare, covering Hospital Insurance, Medical Insurance, Medicare Advantage Plans and Prescription Drug coverage.


Premera

Premera is a not for profit Blue Cross Blue Shield licensed health insurance company. They provide health insurance primarily in Washington state (apart from Clark County). With over 80 years experience in the field and approximately 2 million people insured.

They offer medical and dental plans. Those over 65 years old can obtain a better price on medical plans.

They also provide family or group plans for employers.

Kaiser Permanente

Kaiser Permanente is a health insurance company that originates from Oakland, California. As of 2017, they had 11.7 million health plan members. There are two entities of Kaiser Permanente, a non-profit and for profit sections. Their recent net income came in at $72.7 billion in operating revenue.
